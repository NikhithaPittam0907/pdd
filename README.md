LexisAI – AI-Powered LegalTech Platform
📌 Overview
LexisAI is an AI-powered legal case management and assistance platform designed to connect Clients, Lawyers, Police Officers, and Administrators through a unified digital ecosystem. The platform streamlines legal workflows, case tracking, document analysis, compliance verification, and AI-driven legal assistance.
